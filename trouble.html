trouble
origin: chicago, illinois
formed: 1978
genre: pioneering doom metal band, some psychadelic metal
more: their metal archive page

these guys are righteous beyond belief. i stumbled across some demo recordings of them and was instantly hooked. if you know me you know i'm not super into speed metal or anything overly thrash, i usually prefer something slower and grittier, but i still don't mind some album diversity and i feel like trouble accomplishes metal almost perfectly suited to my tastes, which is crazy. as far as typical big four doom metal og's, i put them right above saint vitus in my go-to regiment. give them a try and say hello to doom heaven.

start with some of these:
the tempter
psalm 9
at psychotic reaction
come together (which is a beatles cover)
flowers

a few favorites:
run to the light
death wish 1 (83 bootleg)
memory's garden
the wish
after the rain
