concrete blonde
from: hollywood babyyy
formed: 1982
genre: alt rock, gothic rock, post-punk
from the mid-80's through the 90's, johnette napolitano sang liquid gold with this band-- which has so much more to offer than just their hits; but i mean, come on, no one really strikes the whole melody and punch balance more effortlessly than CONCRETE BLONDE. also any group that covers george harrison has reached enlightenment. oh, i was also born around their second / last group breakup in june of 06, so i'm fairly sure i'm this band reincarnated. listen to bloodletting and stare at your ceiling waiting for nothing and you'll understand them. man, she's such a crazy talented vocalist, and not many lead hits stay fresh to death like joey has, so there's something to be said for that. their harmonies are legit, they're legit, try and give them a shot.
