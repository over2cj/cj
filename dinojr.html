dinosaur jr.
origin: they're massholes   |   formed: 1984   |   genre: indie and noise rock, hardcore punk   |   status: back together
band info
formative influence on alt rock, with unique vocals and a fair share of band drama. mike johnson was lou barlow's replacement, barlow going on to make the pretty great band sebadoh. you can tell they want to kill each other a bit during old stuff, which adds to the art.
my take on it
well i love them plently. i first heard 'hide' driving home from work one day and my life was changed, i looped it till the driveway. they're just so far beyond. there's something so gorgeous about their perspectives and musicality.
