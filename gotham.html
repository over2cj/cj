about them
it's a shame their official output was so tentative, because it's hard to find deep cuts of their music. this band is tight, definitely a favorite in the heavy metal sphere. what can i say, it's either for you or it ain't, so give it a shot if you're into the occasional sword-bearing hard guitar palette cleasner.
