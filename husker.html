hüsker dü
band info
3 member minnesota-formed band active from 79-88. the name comes from an old 50's board game.
what i think
the prettiest punk rock turned alt rock ever. some of their lyrics hit like no other. an incredible sound for the 80's especially, way ahead of their time. to me they're right at home on a playlist with concrete blonde, which is a huge plus.
