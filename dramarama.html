NO RELATION TO THAT MFKNG SHOW. sort of a black sheep in the mix of 80's bands, because they didn't quite conform to the extremity of rock fashion or production at the timne, but also kept outside the circle of indie pop and other more cutesy sounds. they've cycled through a serious amount of members, but their origins were real simple: working at a hardware store and writing breathtaking songs with a storytelling edge, almost like a lot of their work can be considered an entire narrative within each respective album. they escape corporate garbage noise entirely, but also have catchy and memorable music, so give them a try.
